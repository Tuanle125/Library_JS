7 Habits
The 7 Habits by Stephen Covey provide a framework for effectiveness and success through habits like taking initiative instead of reacting, envisioning your goals and purpose, prioritizing important tasks, fostering cooperation over competition, understanding others' viewpoints before being understood, leveraging diversity through synergizing talents, and consistently renewing and developing yourself through learning. These interconnected habits cultivate a proactive mindset and continuous self-improvement.
how to win friends and influence people
Dale Carnegie's timeless bestseller offers principles for building strong relationships and gaining influence over others. He advocates becoming genuinely interested in other people rather than talking mostly about yourself. His teachings include remembering names, smiling, paying sincere compliments, and showing sincere appreciation. Carnegie encourages focusing on others' interests and making them feel important. He stresses avoiding criticism and arguments, which breed ill will. Instead, one should emphasize understanding different viewpoints. Overall, the book provides practical tips for improving social skills, being a good conversationalist, and positively influencing people through kindness, empathy and making them feel valued.
